fdevlib-example: Suggestion

1、默认状态

调用代码示意：


						 FD.widget.Suggest('suggestion_1');

2、通过result参数改变列表

调用代码示意：


						 FD.widget.Suggest('suggestion_2','',{resultFormat:'共有%result%条',showCloseBtn:true});

3、更换数据源，和返回值变量名

调用代码示意：


						 FD.widget.Suggest('suggestion_3','http://suggest.china.alibaba.com/bin/suggest',{resultFormat:'',varName:'_suggest_result_'});

4、设定显示条数，超过限制出现滚动条

调用代码示意：


						 FD.widget.Suggest('suggestion_4','',{resultFormat:'',showNum:8});
